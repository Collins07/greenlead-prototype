GRI 305: Emissions

Provide data emissions from various sources, such as combustion, transportation, and supply chain activities, and implements strategies to reduce emissions and mitigate climate change.

These are direct emissions from sources that you own or control, such as emissions from owned or leased vehicles, equipment, and facilities.


These are indirect emissions associated with the generation of purchased electricity, steam, heating, and cooling consumed by the organization.


These emissions can include activities such as business travel, employee commuting, supply chain emissions, and the use of sold products.
